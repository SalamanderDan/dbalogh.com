Screamer

Android app for dropping your phone

Screamer Description:

Screamer is an interactive soundboard app that plays sounds when you move your phone, such as screaming when you drop it, or lawnmower sounds when you pull it back.

CodeDay Description:

The Scream Team built Screamer in just 24 hours at CodeDay Chicago. CodeDay is an event to encourage young people to code, by locking us in a room for 24 hours and seeing what comes out. To learn more, go to Code Day's site!

Example Gestures:

The Drop:

Registers when the phone is falling.



The Shake:

Registers when the phone is shaken around.



The Upward Toss:

Registers when the phone is tossed upwards.



The Conductor:

Registers when the phone is waved from side to side.



The Barrell Roll:

Registers when the phone is rotated across its y axis.



The Lawnmower:

Registers when the phone is pulled back like a lawn mower.



Pop Goes the Weasel:

Activates when the phone is slowly rotated around the z axis.
